About Bharathiyar

Mahakavi Subramania Bharathi was a great Tamil poet, freedom fighter, journalist, and social reformer. His poems inspired people to fight against British rule and social injustice.

Life Timeline

  • 1882: Born in Ettayapuram
  • 1897: Received title "Bharathi"
  • 1904: Journalist at Swadesamitran
  • 1908: Moved to Puducherry
  • 1918: Returned to India
  • 1921: Passed away

Famous Poems

  • Achamillai Achamillai
  • Pudhumai Penn
  • Panchali Sabatham
  • Kuyil Pattu
  • Sudesa Geethangal

Legacy

Bharathiyar is remembered as the Father of Modern Tamil Poetry. His thoughts on freedom, women empowerment, and equality continue to inspire generations.
